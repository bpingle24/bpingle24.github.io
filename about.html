Background

Propulsion R&D, from first principles up.

Hello! My name is Blake Pingleton, and I'm a junior studying Aeronautical & Astronautical Engineering at Purdue University. The mindset I’ve cultivated over the last few years of experience led me to a single working philosophy: start from the roots.

Investigating our own assumptions and biases toward an engineering problem is sometimes the greatest constraint. Before we can attempt to solve a problem, we must first understand the underlying physics, the solution we’re trying to achieve, and what stands in the way. Only then can we expect to produce robust and reliable designs.

My goals for the future begin with earning a professional degree, then transitioning into academic or industry research and development focused on propulsion systems.

"The first principle is that you must not fool yourself — and you are the easiest person to fool."
